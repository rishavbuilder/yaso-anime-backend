# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Anime enthusiasts who browse late at night, seeking curated content rather than algorithmic feeds. They value editorial taste, atmospheric aesthetics, and a meditative viewing experience. Primary audience: dedicated anime fans who appreciate curation over volume. Secondary: casual viewers drawn in by the unique aesthetic.

## Product Purpose

A curated night-shift anime streaming archive. No algorithms — just stories worth staying up for. Success looks like users returning nightly, trusting the archive's taste, and discovering anime they wouldn't have found elsewhere. The site becomes their midnight ritual.

## Positioning

The archive watches after midnight — a hand-stamped collection of anime, presented with Japanese ink-wash reverence.

## Brand Personality

Zen & contemplative. Japanese ink-wash (sumi-e) aesthetic, wabi-sabi imperfection, meditative pace. Voice is quiet confidence, not hype. The interface should feel like entering a dimly lit room with tatami mats — calm, intentional, beautiful in restraint.

## Anti-references

- **Generic anime sites** (Crunchyroll, Funimation): cluttered grids, algorithmic recommendations, bright colors, overwhelming choice
- **Mainstream streaming** (Netflix, Disney+): corporate polish, homogenized UI, zero personality
- **Ad-heavy sites**: visual noise, pop-ups, distracting banners

## Design Principles

1. **Curated over complete** — Less is more; every featured anime earned its spot
2. **Atmosphere is content** — The visual mood IS part of the experience, not decoration
3. **Quiet confidence** — Let the work speak; no loud CTAs or algorithmic urgency
4. **Ritualistic UX** — Design for nightly returns, not one-time visits
5. **Japanese reverence** — Typography, spacing, and motion should honor the source material

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA compliance
- Reduced motion support (prefers-reduced-motion)
- Keyboard navigation for all features
- Semantic HTML structure
- Screen reader friendly labels
- High contrast text on dark backgrounds
